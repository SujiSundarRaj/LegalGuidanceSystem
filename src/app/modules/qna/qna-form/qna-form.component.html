Tips for asking questions
  • Include Essential Details: Share pertinent information, but avoid overloading with unnecessary details.
  • Be Concise: Craft your question to the point, avoiding unnecessary elaboration.
  • Follow Up: If needed, ask follow-up questions to gain a complete understanding of your options.
  • Respectful Language: Frame your questions in a respectful and courteous manner, fostering positive communication.
